The Best Movies According to Shubham

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Harry Potter

Harry Potter is my favorite because it's a timeless tale of magic, friendship, and the power of love that transports you to a world where anything is possible.

Iron Man

Really cool sci-fi movie.

Spiderman

Spider-Man is my favorite because his story embodies the essence of heroism, showing that even ordinary people can make a difference when faced with extraordinary challenges.
